Confused on how to get the Crypto you want to track? Follow these steps below.

Step 1:

Visit Messari

Step 2:

Locate this section on the bottom left of the screen:

Step 3:

Click any one of these Cryptos or click on the next page to see more and find one you want to track.

Step 4:

After finding an asset you want to track, click on it.

Step 5:

Check the URL. You should see something like "https://messari.io/asset/xrp". In this case, the ticker name is "xrp" (the last part of the URL).

Step 6:

Copy the ticker name and head back to "Edit Crypto List" and paste it into the field.

Step 7:

Click "Fetch"!
